Scrollytelling

Scrollytelling has shut down.

31 December 2018 was our last day.
It's been an incredible journey.

We are working on archiving all the things, and will report back here when you can look. Now stop crying!

Scroll For Dear God why

Walking away from a good thing

It's not for lack of success: we enabled 900+ stories to be produced serving 7 million viewers. But lots and lots of small things wore us down. And instead of having to stay the course at all cost, we chose to gently exit and move on.

Stability

We are committed to keeping all stories online on their current locations. They just won't be editable any longer. Instead, they shall live on as static HTML until the Sun becomes a Red Giant and swallows Earth.

Take your data

We've also made arrangements with customers who prefer receiving the raw data. It's all good. We're not animals.

Burning questions?

Or just ready to sling us a compliment, or even a complaint? Don't hold back. We-Joost and Martijn-are still here, minding the shop. Email us, we'd love to hear from ya.

Archives

We've gone through a painstaking amount of trouble in preserving all stories told using our platform. In this section you'll find links to the stories in all their glory. But also a cornucopia of metadata, such as the people involved, dates, and technologies used. In various formats and feeds, there is bound to be something there for you.

We even produced screenshots of every single page, so that even as browsers change and make the code obsolete, you'll still be able to enjoy the stories in their intended form. To seal the deal, the archives are served as self-contained folders of HTML and nothing more. Unwrap them on any web server and you're good to go.
